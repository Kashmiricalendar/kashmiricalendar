Information Collection and Usage

Kashmiri Hindu Calendar is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app provides cultural calendar information and event details without requiring personal data collection. The app may request notification permissions to send you reminders about upcoming cultural events and festivals. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve transmitting your personal information to external servers.

The app does not access, store, or transmit your contacts, location data, or other personal information. All cultural calendar data and event information is provided through the app without requiring personal identification.

Data Storage and Security

Kashmiri Hindu Calendar stores cultural calendar data and event information locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your personal information. Any app preferences or settings you configure are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to external servers.
